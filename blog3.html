Budget-Friendly Travel Destinations 💸

Who says travel has to be expensive? Here are five destinations where your money goes a long way.

1. Vietnam 🇻🇳
From delicious street food to beautiful beaches, Vietnam offers a full experience for backpackers on a budget.

2. Portugal 🇵🇹
Charming towns, surf spots, and wine tours—all without breaking the bank.

3. Mexico 🇲🇽
Explore the culture, beaches, and street tacos of Mexico with affordable accommodations and local buses.

4. Nepal 🇳🇵
A dream for trekkers! Hike the Himalayas, visit temples, and stay in inexpensive guesthouses.

5. Albania 🇦🇱
Underrated and stunning, Albania has gorgeous beaches and mountain views at a fraction of Europe’s typical cost.

Start planning now and enjoy unforgettable adventures without draining your wallet!
